Halloumi Tikka Masala

Description

The beloved tikka masala sauce with halloumi cheese instead of chicken.

Ingredients

Spice Base

  • 1 Onion
  • 4 Cloves of Garlic
  • 1 tbsp of Minced Ginger
  • 0,25-0,5 tsp Chili Flakes - Optional
  • 2 tsp Garam Massala
  • 2 tsp Tumeric
  • 2 tsp Paprika
  • 2 tsp Ground Coriander
  • 2 tsp Cumin
  • 1 tsp Salt

Furthermore

  • 1 dl yoghurt 3,5%
  • 400 g Halloumi Cheese
  • 3 tbsp Butter
  • 3 tbsp Tomato Paste
  • 400 g Canned Tomatoes Finely Chopped
  • 2 dl Water
  • 1 cube Vegetable Stock
  • 2,5 dl Cream
  • 1 Cauliflower
  • 4 dl Rice
  • Black Pepper

Steps

  1. Turn on your oven to 200 degrees celsius.
  2. Boil some water in a pot and start cooking your rice.
  3. Chop cauliflower into bite-sized pieces. Put the chopped cauliflower on a baking sheet with oil, salt and pepper. Add the cauliflower to your oven and bake for 20 minutes.
  4. Peel the onion and chop it roughly. Put it in a blender with garlic, ginger, chili flakes, the spices. Also, add a small amount of water or oil. Blend until a fine paste.
  5. Mix a third of the spice paste with 1 dl yoghurt. Dice the halloumi cheese into 2 cm dices. Put the halloumi cheese in the yoghurt mix.
  6. Melt butter in a medium sized pot. Add the remaining spice paste and cook at medium heat for 5 minutes.
  7. Add tomato paste and cook for 5 more minutes.
  8. Add the canned tomatoes, water, vegetable stock and cream. Let the dish simmer for 20-30 minutes until the sauce has thickened.
  9. Heat a pan with oil on high heat. Add the halloumi cheese, but not the yoghurt marinade, to the hot pan. When the halloumi cheese is seared add it to the sauce.
  10. Add the rest of the yoghurt marinade to the sauce.
  11. Add the baked cauliflower to the sauce and stir.
  12. Serve with rice.
