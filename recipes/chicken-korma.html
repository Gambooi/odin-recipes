Chicken Korma

Description

The classic indian dish with chicken, almonds and lovely spices.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp Sunflower Oil or Butter
  • 1 Onion, peeled and finely chopped
  • 400 g Chicken Breast, chopped into bite-sized pieces
  • 2 Cloves of Garlic, peeled and minced
  • 2 tsp Minced Ginger
  • 1 tsp Ground Cumin
  • 1,5 tsp Ground Coriander
  • 1,5 tsp Garam Masala
  • 0,5 tsp Mild Chili Powder
  • 0,5 tsp Turmeric
  • 0,25 tsp Salt
  • 0,25 Black Pepper
  • 2 tbsp Tomato Puree
  • 120 ml Greek Yoghurt
  • 1 can of Coconut Cream
  • 2 tbsp Ground Almonds
  • 1 tbsp Sugar
  • 4 tbsp Double Cream

Steps

  1. Boil some water in a pot and start cooking your rice.
  2. Heat the oil or butter in a large frying pan over medium heat.
  3. Add the onion and cook for 5 minutes until softened. Stir often.
  4. Add the chicken breast and cook for 5 minutes.
  5. Add the garlic, ginger, cumin, ground coriander, garam masala, chili powder, turmeric, salt, pepper and tomato puree.
  6. Cook for 2 minutes while stirring.
  7. Add yoghurt, coconut cream, ground almonds and sugar.
  8. Stir everything together and bring to a gentle boil. Let it simmer for 6-8 minutes.
  9. Stir in the double cream and turn off the heat.
  10. Serve with rice.
