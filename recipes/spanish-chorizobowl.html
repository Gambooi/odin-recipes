Spanish Chorizobowl

Description

Enjoy a dish with fregola and Spanish chorizo sausage.

Ingredients

  • 550 g Cauliflower
  • 1 Feta Cheese
  • 0,5 tsp Paprika
  • 0,5 tsp Ground Cumin
  • 0,5 tsp Black Pepper
  • 0,5 tsp Garlic Powder
  • 0,5 tsp Onion Powder
  • 0,5 tsp Salt
  • 0,5 tsp Thyme
  • 0,5 tsp Oregano
  • 400 g Chorizo Sausage
  • 335 g Fregola
  • 25 ml Balsamico
  • 1 cube of Vegetable Stock
  • 2 Red Onions
  • 2 Carrots
  • 60 g Spinach
  • 2 tbsp Sugar

Steps

  1. Turn on the oven at 220 degrees celsius.
  2. Mix paprika, ground cumin, black pepper, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, thyme and oregano.
  3. Dice carrots into 1 cm cubes. Cut cauliflower into bite-sized pieces. Put on a baking sheet, add the spice mix and drizzle with olive oil. Mix together.
  4. Bake the carrots and cauliflower in the oven for 15-20 minutes.
  5. Add vegetable stock to 8 dl of water in a pot. Bring to a boil.
  6. Add fregola in the pot and lower the temperature to medium. Let it simmer for 12-15 minutes.
  7. Cut the chorizo sausage into 1 cm cubes. Slice red onions into thin halfmoons. Add olive oil to a pan on medium high heat and cook chorizo and red onion for 5 minutes.
  8. Add balsamico, 2 tbsp water and 2 tbsp sugar to the pan. Let it simmer for 3-5 minutes.
  9. Wash spinach and chop roughly.
  10. Mix fregola, spinach, red onions, chorizo sausage, carrots, cauliflower and half of the feta cheese in a big serving bowl.
  11. Top off with chunks of feta cheese.
  12. Serve while warm.
